Phase 3: Architecture Definition

In the third phase of a migration, the architect considers important aspects for the target architecture. For this he chooses the patterns for the target architecture and at the same time, he considers appropriate best practices for this purpose.

As a first step, you may want to explore the entire list of patterns and best practices by clicking on "Explore all architectural designs". This will give you a list of patterns and best practices which can be explored in detail by clicking on them.

Otherwise you can look for recommended patterns and best practices based on qualities, inputs, outputs, processes and usability aspects of existing patterns and best practices by clicking on "Find recommended Patterns and Best Practices".
