Phase 3

In Phase 3 of a migration, the target microservices architecture is designed. Patterns and best practices for the architectural design are means to achieve certain quality goals.

As a first step, you may want to explore the list of cataloged patterns and best practices by clicking on "Explore all Patterns and Best Practices". It will present a list with the possibility to inspect all details of a pattern or best practice. "Find recommended Patterns and Best Practices" presents you a ranked list of patterns based on the filter configuration in Phase 2 (shown in the Sidebar). "Find scenario-based recommended Patterns and Best Practices" will combine the scenarios' quality goals from Phase 1 with the additional filter configuration in Phase 2 for calculating the ranking.
