Phase 1

In the first phase of a migration, this tool supports architects in setting strategic goals, identifying driving forces for the migration and subsequently identifying measurable quality attributes. To do so, the tool collects basic system specifications and guides through an architecture review / assessment (e.g. based on ATAM or SAAM) that can either be scenario-based or solely rely on quality attributes.

Therein, each of the two architectural styles "monolith" and "microservices" will be rated according to their suitability for each scenario or quality attribute. As a result, the tool will present a comparison between the two styles based on the sum of their ratings. The architect can now make a grounded decision pro/contra a migration to microservices and has the metrics at hand that help to measure the migration's success. 2
