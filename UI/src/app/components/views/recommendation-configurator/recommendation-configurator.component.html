Configure a filter for available refactoring approaches

Here you have three possibilities:

"Include" - the approach must have this property
"Neutral" - the approach can have this property
"Exclude" - the approach does not have this property

Here you have the possibility to apply the inputs from the scenarios for the qualities for the configuration. You can see your configuration based on the scenarios in the sidebar on the right.

You have the option of having the recommendations generated exclusively on the basis of the inputs made from the scenarios, or you can make additional configurations. In addition to the qualities, system properties can be added. There is also the possibility to further configure the input preferences, the process preferences, the output preferences and the usability preferences.
