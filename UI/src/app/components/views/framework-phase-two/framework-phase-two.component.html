Phase 2: Strategy Definition

In the second phase of a migration, the architect chooses the migration strategy and subsequently selects a suitable service identification approach. The diagram on the following page shows the different development strategies that can be chosen for a system modernization or migration.

As a first step, you may want to explore the entire list of approaches by clicking on "Explore all approaches". This will give you a list (currently there's only a subset of 18 approaches are in the database) of approaches which can be explored in detail by clicking on them. Each approach represents a peer-reviewed scientific publication.

Otherwise you can look for recommended refactoring approaches based on inputs, outputs, processes and usability aspects of existing refactoring approaches by clicking on "Find recommended approaches".
